  • Eligibility Rules
    • A Candidate is declared as passed in the 1st/2nd Year examination if he/she gets a minimum of 30% marks in each paper and 35% aggregate in one attempt.

    • Minimum pass mark for individual papers of 1st/2nd or both under compartmental appearence is 35%.

    • Blind, Deaf, Deaf & Dumb candidates are declared as passed 1st/2nd Year,if the candidate gets a minimum of 25% marks in each paper.The award of Grade will be as per usual procedure.

    • The Deaf, Deaf & Dumb, Dyslexia and Mentally handicapped candidates are exempted from one language under Part I or Part II.The Grade will be awarded as usual in consideration of papers appeared.

    • The "Environmental Education" examination is compulsory from the academic year 2006-07 and the "Ethics and Human Values" examination is compulsory from the academic year 2014-15.The Candidates will be issued pass certificates only after qualifying the said two subjects.The Blind Candidates are exempted from appearing the Environmental Education Examination only.

  • Greetings to the students passed in Intermediate Examination


  • Dear Student,

  • Congratulations, on your success in Intermediate Public Examination, MARCH-2024

  •          Degree Online Services, Telangana (DOST) welcomes you to join in Undergraduate Courses offered by the Degree colleges across Telangana. DOST (https://dost.cgg.gov.in) is the only Gateway to join in the Degree Courses (B.A./B.Com./B.Sc. etc.) in Telangana.

  • Instructions for UG Degree Online Admission (DOST):-
    • Link the mobile number of student ( or parent) with student's Aadhaar Number.

    • Well in advance (before the beginning of DOST registrations) update your name, gender and date of birth in Aadhaar Card as per your SSC / Intermediate Certificate.

    • Use Student's Aadhaar Number for DOST Registration. Don't use Aadhaar number of Parents / Siblings / Others for Registration.

    • Visit https://dost.cgg.gov.in and register by entering your details and paying Rs.200/- online.

    • One Time Password (OTP) will be sent to your Aadhaar linked mobile number. On receiving OTP, login for further registration process.

    • DOST ID will be generated and this will be valid till the end of the admission process (and even in future also).

    • If applicable, keep the following certificates ready with you : Caste Certificate, Income Certificate (taken on or after 01.04.2023), NCC, PHC, Ex-Servicemen Or any other relevant Certificates obtained from the Competent Authorities.

    • Take the help of your Teachers / Elders while exercising web options of courses / colleges of your choice.

    • For further assistance, contact nearest Help line center, DOST. Don't tell OTP to Unknown or Unauthorised Persons.
